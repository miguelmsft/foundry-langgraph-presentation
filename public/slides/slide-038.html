Thank you all for your time. The key takeaway: building a LangGraph agent and deploying it to Foundry is remarkably simple — create_react_agent for the agent, from_langgraph for the bridge, three config files for packaging, and azd up for deployment. The power is in what you get for free: managed infrastructure, auto-scaling, Entra ID, conversation management, full observability, and one-click publishing to Teams. I'm happy to take questions.
