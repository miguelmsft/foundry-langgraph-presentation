What Foundry Manages For You

  • ⚡ Auto-scaling — 1 to 5 replicas, scale-to-zero supported
  • 🔐 Entra ID Identity — Managed identity pre-publish; dedicated identity post-publish
  • 📊 Telemetry & Tracing — OpenTelemetry → Application Insights, automatic
  • 📢 Publishing — One-click to Teams, M365 Copilot, Entra Agent Registry
  • 🛡️ Enterprise Security — RBAC, content filters, guardrails
Let me walk through what Foundry gives you for free. Auto-scaling between 1 and 5 replicas — you can even set min to 0 for scale-to-zero, but expect cold starts. Identity management is interesting: before you publish, your agent uses the project's system-assigned managed identity. After publishing, Foundry provisions a dedicated identity for your agent — and you need to reassign permissions, which is a gotcha to watch for. Telemetry flows automatically to Application Insights. And publishing is a one-click operation to Teams, M365 Copilot, or a stable API endpoint.
